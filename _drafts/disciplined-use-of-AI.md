### Adjusting your skills portfolio

There are three main ways to move up the value chain as an engineer. You need generalized tech skills plus at least one of these:

- leadership
- deep technical expertise
- domain knowledge

It's a little early to say how AI will shift the relative valuations of those dimensions, but it's a sure bet that AI is draining value from generic tech skills in isolation.

I'm the wrong personality-type to lead. I'm a technical generalist. But, I'm curious and I like playing in other people's backyards. So, I've learned enough biomedical background knowledge to have a real conversation with researchers. That works for me. Given your predispositions, pick your poison.

### Conclusion

A lot more thought needs to go into disciplined use of AI, a thought-out process that yields good outcomes, solid understanding of the problem domain and the technical implimentation.
